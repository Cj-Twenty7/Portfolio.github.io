I was first introduced to this game by a novel. This game called Game of the Generals is a tactical warfare game that simulates a real battlefield in the form of a board game. As it is technically a battlefield, each player must play their pieces as that of a real general commanding his troops in war. It sharpens the players strategy planning, memory, critical thinking and decision making skills. It is a very enjoyable and interesting game to play.
